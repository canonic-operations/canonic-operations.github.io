## Sales Patterns

### Healthcare Enterprise

1. **Long cycle** - 6-18 months typical
2. **Multi-buyer** - IT, Clinical, Compliance, Finance
3. **Pilot required** - Proof before purchase
4. **Budget cycles** - Q4 budget, Q1 execution

### Academic/Research

1. **Grant-funded** - Proposal timeline drives
2. **PI-centric** - Individual champion required
3. **Paper pipeline** - Publications validate
4. **Conference presence** - Visibility matters

### Startup/VC

1. **Speed** - Decisions in days not months
2. **Equity** - Often part of deal structure
3. **Founder access** - Direct line required
4. **Demo-driven** - Show don't tell
