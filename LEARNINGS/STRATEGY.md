## Axiom

**Patterns from execution. Synthesized from TRANSCRIPTS and DEALS.**

---

## Enterprise Sales Pattern

1. **Slow cycles** - Q4 budget extensions common
2. **Multi-stakeholder** - Strategy, research, admin alignment required
3. **Budget clarity** - Resources/assets: people, process, capital/technology
4. **Different path** - When blocked, pivot approach not goal

---

## Network Pattern

1. **Political navigation** - Massage the network, play the game
2. **Legacy activation** - Mentor transitions activate alumni
3. **Conference presence** - Academic venues build credibility
4. **Cross-org** - Same user across orgs = leverage multiplier

---

## Grant Pattern

1. **Agency alignment** - Match org to funder preference
2. **Timing** - SBIR January cycle, account for shutdowns
3. **STTR** - University partnership required, paper pipeline
4. **Diversity** - Studies need representativeness

---

## Startup Pattern

1. **Trademark early** - Before traction, not after
2. **Customer order** - Payors/providers/employers FIRST; patients/pharma AFTER
3. **Equity clarity** - Operating agreement before building
4. **Interview velocity** - 50+ in program validates market

---

## Anti-Patterns

- Facade over substance
- Invested interest without alignment
- Bureaucracy before execution
- Independence without governance
